About

We’ve been making fresh, authentic NY style pizza for over twenty years. We grew up enjoying and perfecting pizza, and we want to share our passion with everyone we serve.

Our high standards aren’t easy to keep, but they’re necessary to create the most delicious, authentic New York Style pizza, subs, and Italian dinners. And that’s what our customers deserve. So no matter who stops in for a slice, we always give them our best.
